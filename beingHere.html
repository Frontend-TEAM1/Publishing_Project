Being Here

Show up, make incredible work, and live
the life you want


From day one, we’ve been committed to making Instrument a place where
people can make the best work of their career — sustainably. This means
working reasonable hours. Spending each day in a welcoming, inclusive
environment. Taking vacations when you need them. We believe in doing
everything we can to help our employees lead healthy, full lives.

  • Health & Wellbeing


  • While it should be standard practice for companies to offer
    health and wellbeing benefits to their employees, it’s not
    always the case. Here, it is—even alternative care such as
    massage and acupuncture. All available to you on your first
    day of employment.


  • Generous coverage for three medical plan
    options (employee cost subject to coverage
    selection)
  • 100% employee coverage vision and dental
    insurance
  • Alternative / naturopathic care benefits
  • Full transgender coverage (including hormone
    therapy and gender affirming surgery)
  • Full fertility coverage
  • Optional Health Savings Account (HSA)
  • Optional Flexible Spending Account (FSA)
  • WFH & Wellbeing monthly stipend
  • Mental Health coverage and support
  • Headspace membership

  • Time Off


  • Work is important, but so is everything else. We want you
    to have time to do all the things that make you, you.


  • Generous Vacation Time Off (starting at 3 weeks,
    increasing with tenure)
  • Sick Leave
  • Learning Time Off
  • Volunteer Time Off
  • Team Days Off (for your project team to take a
    break together)
  • Company-wide closures (for the whole company to
    take a break together)

  • Professional Growth


  • Your growth is important to us. Expanding your skills,
    perspective, and experiences are all supported here.


  • Structured career management and mentorship
  • Internal programming & education
  • Individual professional development stipend

  • Profit Sharing


  • It’s simple. You help us reach our goals; we share the
    rewards with you.


  • Every full-time employee receives a
    discretionary profit-sharing bonus each year

  • Retirement


  • Were we ever to use the term ‘adulting’—which we
    wouldn’t—it would be for this. Planning ahead is smart,
    and we’d like to help set you up for a great future.


  • 100% 401K matching for up to 4% of your eligible
    compensation

  • Parental leave


  • We get it—growing a family is wonderful and exhausting.
    Many of us are parents, too. We respect your time away,
    and will support you as you transition back to work. These
    benefits apply to the birth, adoption or foster care
    placement of a new human in your life.


  • 16 weeks paid & protected leave for primary
    caregivers
  • 8 additional weeks protected leave for primary
    caregivers (unpaid)
  • 4-6 weeks paid leave for secondary caregivers
  • Dedicated, private lactation rooms in our offices
  • Contribution for after‑school care, daycare, and
    in‑home care for dependents

  • Hardship leave


  • It’s important to us that we take care of you if
    something serious comes up for you or someone close
    to you.


  • Medical, bereavement, hardship, and other types
    of leave
